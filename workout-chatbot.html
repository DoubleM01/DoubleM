About the Project

The **Workout Chatbot** project is designed to provide users with personalized workout plans and fitness advice through an interactive chatbot interface. The chatbot leverages natural language processing and machine learning techniques to understand user queries and offer tailored responses.

Collaborators: [List of collaborators]

Features

  • Personalized Workout Plans: Generate customized workout routines based on user preferences and fitness goals.
  • Real-time Feedback: Provide instant feedback and suggestions during workouts.
  • Progress Tracking: Track user progress and adjust workout plans accordingly.
  • Nutrition Advice: Offer dietary recommendations to complement workout routines.

Target Users

The primary users are fitness enthusiasts and individuals looking to improve their physical health through guided workout routines and personalized fitness advice.
